Baked eggs in avocado

Description

Packed with protein, hardboiled eggs are a smart choice for paleo lunches. "I wanted to make a healthier egg salad," says Karen. "This one uses avocado instead of mayonnaise."

Ingredients

  • 3 hard boiled eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tbsp sweet pickle relish
  • 1 tbsp chopped onion
  • 1 tbsp chopped celery
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. peel,pit and mash avocado
  2. peel and chop eggs
  3. mix all items in bowl
  4. optional - Serve with carrier of choice (celery, crackers, sandwich bread, etc.)
